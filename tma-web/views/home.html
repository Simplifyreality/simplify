  • If you are OBSM you can:
    • Read journey logs, filter them according to your needs and export the data in JOURNEY LOGS,
    • Concentrate on issues reported in journey logs and filter and export the data in ISSUES,
    • Send messages to one or several trains, live or in advance, view and participate in live chats with the TMs, in E-MESSAGES,
    • Read about faults as they get reported in CARNET DE BORD,
    • Write, manage and send temporary announcements to the relevant trains in SPECIALS,
    • Create, manage and send train objectives in TRAIN OBJECTIVES,
    • Manage user accounts, view personalised announcements and reset passwords in ADMIN,
    • Review and change standard announcements in ADMIN.
  • If you are CRE you can:
    • Send messages to one or several trains, live or in advance, receive notifications of Dépêches, view and participate in live chats with the TMs, in E-MESSAGES,
    • Read about faults as they are being reported in CARNET DE BORD,
    • Read journey logs, filter them according to your needs and export the data in JOURNEY LOGS,
    • concentrate on issues reported in journey logs and filter and export the data in ISSUES.
  • If you are Depot you can:
    • Read about faults as they get reported, sort and filter them according to your needs, assign a status and comment on them in CARNET DE BORD.
  • If you are admin you can:
    • Read journey logs, filter them according to your needs and export the data in JOURNEY LOGS,
    • Concentrate on issues reported in journey logs and filter and export the data in ISSUES,
    • Read about faults as they get reported in CARNET DE BORD.
